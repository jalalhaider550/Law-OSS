Unlimited uncapped warranty liability is non-market. Use Contract Agent to generate redline positions.
Uncapped Warranty LiabilityCRITICAL
No financial cap and no time limitation on warranty claims. Market standard for UK M&A of this size is 100% of consideration cap (£94.5M) with 18-month general survival and 7-year tax warranty period.
Suggest: Cap at 100% of consideration, 18-month general survival, 7-year tax, £100k basket/de minimis. Standard Travers Smith market positions.
21-Day Completion PeriodHIGH
Completion in 21 days is unrealistic. FCA change of control notification takes minimum 60 working days. CMA merger review may be triggered. Sellers cannot satisfy CPs in this timeframe.
Suggest: Long-stop date of 31 July 2025 with automatic 30-day extension if regulatory CPs remain outstanding. Consider reverse break fee.
No Locked Box / Completion AccountsHIGH
Agreement is silent on price adjustment mechanism. For pharma acquisitions of this value, parties must agree locked box (with economic date) or completion accounts mechanism.
Suggest: Locked box with economic date 31 December 2024; include permitted leakage schedule with agreed categories.
Non-Compete — Likely UnenforceableMEDIUM
Europe-wide 5-year non-compete is likely void under the restraint of trade doctrine (Nordenfelt v Maxim [1894] AC 535) and potentially contrary to Chapter I competition prohibition.
Suggest: UK and Ireland only, 2-year term, competing products only. 5 years is very likely to be unenforceable.
